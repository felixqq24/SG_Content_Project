The person behind Riviera & Ridge

One qualified person. A rare combination.

A staatlich geprüfte Erzieherin (state-certified early-childhood educator) with the rare combination of ski-instructor competence and a long yoga practice. The training shows in calm rooms. The mothering shows in the way children settle.

Children are different. Some need movement before they can sit still. Some need quiet before they can speak. Some need a clear frame more than they need a programme. That is not a diagnosis — that is attention.

  • State-certified early-childhood educator
  • Certified ski instructor
  • Yoga teacher (3HO, ~400 h)
  • Children's yoga & yoga coach
  • Forest-kindergarten experience
  • Mother of four
