The person behind Auranne

Thirty-four years of mothering — and the discipline to do it well for someone else.

Auranne is the work of one woman: a state-certified early-childhood educator with the rare combination of ski-instructor competence and a long yoga practice. The training shows in calm rooms. The mothering shows in the way children settle.

She works on the basis of polyvagal theory — not as a label, but as a way of meeting children whose nervous systems need a different tempo. There are no diagnoses on this website. There is a way of being present.

  • Certified ski instructor (Swiss Snow Sports)
  • Yoga teacher (3HO, ~400 h)
  • Children's yoga & yoga coach
  • Forest-kindergarten experience
  • Mother of four
