## How we work with agencies

### The qualifying step — always

> Every placement — whether booked via calendar, form, or phone — includes a qualifying step. For new agencies, this is the introductory call. For returning partners, it is the placement briefing. No engagement begins without a mutual confirmation of fit.

### Collaboration model

- Single placements, relief cover, or recurring engagements (e.g. annual ski season).
- One point of contact on each side. No intermediary layers.
- Full transparency on availability, qualifications, and indicative rates.
- Written engagement summary after each placement (hand-over notes, close-out).

### Reciprocal visibility

- Logo placement on both sides, subject to written agreement.
- Agencies may reference `Riviera & Ridge` in their talent portfolio.
- `Riviera & Ridge` may list partner agencies on the website (after mutual agreement).

## UX & Layout Notes (für Tech-Umsetzung)

- **Mobile-first**: Optionen-Grid stackt vertikal; Kalender-Widget volle Breite.
- **Scroll-Anchors**: Jede Sektion (A/B/C) hat eine Scroll-ID, damit die Karten-CTAs direkt dorthin springen.
- **Kalender-Widget**: Cal.com bevorzugt (Open Source, DSGVO-fähig mit EU-Hosting, kostenloser Tier reicht für 1 Person). Calendly als Fallback. Muss in Navy/Pearl gestylt werden können.
- **Form-Backend**: Netlify Built-in Forms (`data-netlify="true"` Attribut) — kein eigener Backend-Code nötig. Submissions im Netlify Dashboard einsehbar.
- **CSRF-Schutz**: Netlify Built-in Forms haben eigenen CSRF-Schutz. Für Custom-Endpoints: Astro CSRF Middleware.
- **Spam-Schutz**: Netlify Built-in Spam Filter (Akismet) + Honeypot-Feld (`data-netlify-honeypot`). Bei Bedarf: Cloudflare Turnstile als zusätzliche Schicht.
- **Analytics-Events**: `contact_form_submit`, `callback_request`, `calendar_booking` als Custom Events — **nur nach Analytics-Consent feuern** (nicht bei Essential-only).
- **Accessibility**: Alle Formularfelder mit `<label>`, Fehlermeldungen mit `aria-live="polite"`, Fokus-Management nach Submit.
